FUSION & FURY!

Seven years after the Cell Games, Goku returns to Earth for the 25th World Martial Arts Tournament. The peace is shattered when the wizard Babidi and his subordinate, the Demon King Dabura, attempt to harness the energy required to resurrect the ancient, unstoppable threat: **Majin Buu**.

During the confrontation, **Vegeta willingly falls under Babidi's control** to regain his ruthless edge, leading to a destructive battle against Goku that provides the final surge of energy needed to awaken Buu. The pink monster proves to be far more powerful and unpredictable than anyone anticipated.

The Z Fighters introduce desperate new tactics, including the **Fusion Dance**, creating **Gotenks**, and the Potara Earrings, leading to the creation of the invincible warrior **Vegito**. Ultimately, the fate of the universe rests on a newly revived Goku's ability to defeat the pure-evil **Kid Buu** with a massive Spirit Bomb.
