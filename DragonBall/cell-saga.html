THE CELL GAMES!

Trunks, the mysterious warrior from the future, arrives to warn the Z Fighters about the impending threat of powerful Androids created by Dr. Gero. While training, the heroes discover that a bio-synthetic monster named **Cell** has arrived from the future to absorb Androids 17 and 18 to achieve his Perfect Form.

The Z Fighters use the **Hyperbolic Time Chamber** to push past their limits. **Vegeta achieves Super Saiyan Grade 2**, but his arrogance allows Cell to complete his transformation.

**Perfect Cell** announces the **Cell Games**, a martial arts tournament to decide the fate of Earth. Goku sacrifices himself to save the planet, and **Gohan** unleashes his dormant power, achieving **Super Saiyan 2** to finally defeat Cell.
