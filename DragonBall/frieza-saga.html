The Z Fighters travel to Planet Namek in search of the original Dragon Balls to resurrect their fallen friends. They arrive only to find themselves in a three-way conflict against Vegeta and the tyrannical space emperor Frieza and his army.

Frieza's elite forces, the Ginyu Force, arrive and push the heroes to their limits, forcing another uneasy alliance between Goku, Gohan, Krillin, and Vegeta.

The conflict climaxes in a desperate battle between Goku and Frieza. Upon witnessing Krillin's death, Goku transforms into the legendary Super Saiyan.

The planet Namek is destroyed, but Goku defeats Frieza, leaving him for dead. The Namekians and the Z Fighters are wished back to Earth, preparing for Frieza's inevitable return.
